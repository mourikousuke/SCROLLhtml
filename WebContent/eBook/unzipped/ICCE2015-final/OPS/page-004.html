Figure 3. System design

Figure 3 shows the system design of the proposed system. Learners use the EPUB reader in class, and

they learn with Android SCROLL outside the class. Learners’ actions — such as opening a book,

zooming, and page turning — are recorded into SCROLL’s server. The proposed system would provide

quizzes or e-book information through Android SCROLL when learners complete specific actions, such

as opening a page that the word the learner has learned outside the class is written, or the learner records

something s/he has learned in class.
