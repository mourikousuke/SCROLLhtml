For example, when a learner opens an EPUB about machines, the learner opens the EPUB

reader from SCROLL for Android first (Figure 4 - 1). Figure 4 - a shows SCROLL for Android.

SCROLL has menu, and the EPUB reader can be opened from there. Then the learner can turn the pages

freely (Figure 4 - 2). Figure 4 - b and Figure 4 - c shows the EPUB reader. Specifically, Figure 4 - b

shows before the page is turned, and Figure 4 - c shows after the page is turned. And if the learner has

5.Conclusions and future work

This paper proposes a seamless learning system with EPUB. This system runs on Android mobile

phones. Learners’ record their learning from EPUB in class and their experiences outside of class

through this system. Then, the system allows learners to relate what they have learned in class and what

they have experienced outside of class by providing quizzes or book information.

In future work, this seamless learning system needs to be evaluated regarding its efficacy and

usability through learners’ actual use. In addition, the verification of the effectiveness of this system

will help improve e-books and the system itself is expected to improve through an analysis of the logs

recorded within the system. Also, we will consider analyzing various methods such as social network

analysis and visualization of graph theory (Mouri et al., 2014; 2015).
